What are tappables?

Introducing tappables

The most important item in Minecraft Earth are tappables. Tappables are your best friend when it comes to collecting new items.

Tappables are collectible items in Minecraft Earth, which can be found randomly in the real world.

To collect a tappable, just tap on the item once youâ€™re in range. Everyone shares the same tappables. For instance if you and a nearby friend open the same chest, you'll both get the exact same items!

PRO TIP!

Every tappable you collect gains you experience points, which helps you to level up. This is the easiest and best way to level up early on in the game!

But take note that after a certain amount of time a tappable may disappear, and so it's best to tap them as soon as you're in range.
