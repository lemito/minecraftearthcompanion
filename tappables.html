What are tappables?

Introducing tappables

Tappables are collectible items in Minecraft Earth, which can be found randomly in the real world.

They can come in the form of mobs, blocks and chests, and are indicated by a white outline.

On top of regular Minecraft mobs, such as sheeps and cows, special types can also be found. For example the muddy pig, which you saw on the last page.

Chests give you multiple items, and usually contain building blocks such as wood and stone.

All tappables can be collected by everyone. For instance if you and a nearby friend open the same chest, you'll both get the exact same items.
