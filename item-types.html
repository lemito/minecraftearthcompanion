Types of items

In Minecraft Earth you can collect items through tappables, as you read on the last page. These tappables have different levels of rarity, which shown by the colour of their outline.

  • White - Common
  • Green - Uncommon
  • Blue - Rare
  • Purple - Epic

Ontop of being able to collect blocks, you can also collect mobs You can then place these mobs down in your world, and they'll move around just as you'd expect!

Special mob variants are found through clicking on an ordinary looking mob of that species. For instance, to collect a muddy pig you must find a pig tappable, and then get lucky!

Water and lava flow, and fires spread, so be careful to not burn down any of your creations! You won't be able to get destroyed blocks back, and you'll have to find them all over again!
