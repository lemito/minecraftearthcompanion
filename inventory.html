Your character and inventory

All about your inventory

Your inventory is where you store all your blocks, just like in regular Minecraft. However, in Minecraft Earth, your mobs will be stored here too!

You can either place blocks from your inventory, or trade them in for XP. The more XP you have, the more you can unlock!

Your character

Your player in Minecraft Earth indicates where you are in the real world. The houses and streets around you are all shown on your screen!

The circle around your player shows what's in range. If something is inside the circle, you can tap on it to interact with it!

You can also of course change your skin. All skins which you've unlocked on Xbox can be used in Minecraft Earth, plus many more!
